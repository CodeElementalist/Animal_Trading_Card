Red Panda

Red panda has two layers of fur to keep itself warm in cold weather.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Length: 59 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years
  • Habitat: Rainy mountain forests

An acrobatic animal with reddish-brown fur that live on trees. It has an adorable kitten-like face that has white-lined ears, white markings on the side of the head and above its small eyes, and mostly white muzzle. Its belly and limbs are black. Its long, bushy, ringed tail serve it as perfect wraparound blanket for warmth.
